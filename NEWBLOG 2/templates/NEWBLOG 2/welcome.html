Paw Point

Mission statement

At PawPoint, our mission is to create a trusted community-driven platform where people can share and discover valuable information that improves the lives of animals and their human companions. From risk assessments of animal shelters to reviews of pet-friendly restaurants, hotels, and public spaces, we strive to empower individuals to make informed, compassionate choices. By combining transparency, advocacy, and accessibility, PawPoint seeks to protect animals, promote ethical care, and build a world where every pet and shelter animal can thrive alongside supportive communities.
